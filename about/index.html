About Myself

Nice to meet you

  • Hi, my name is HuaiYuan Jing. I am a sophomore at the University of Wisconsin Madison, studying Computer Science at the College of Letters and Science.
  • I grew up in Jiangsu, China, learned computer science from middle school, and attended CS competitions for six years. It gave me the confidence to choose CS as my major.
  • Last year, I participated in the ICPC regional competition with my two teammates. We used algorithms and data structures to solve problems and took the 13th place.
  • In the summer, I started AI by self-learning and got the certification of TensorFlow developer. I finished a series of classes on Coursera and practiced on Kaggle. Finally, I took the Google TensorFlow Certification test and completed the learning stage. I believe it is a new start for me in AI.
  • At the university, I decided to take more AI and OS classes and attend competitions in the future. I am very interested in applying AI technology in OS design. I hope to work in a leading company in related fields, like Microsoft or Palantir, when I graduate.
